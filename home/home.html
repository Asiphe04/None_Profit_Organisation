Giving is Virtue.

Many people in the local townships of Cape Town are struggling and sleeping without food in their stomach.

Love

Love is a complex and multifaceted emotion that is often difficult to define. It can manifest in many different ways, such as the love between romantic partners, the love between family members, or the love between friends.

Compassion

Compassion is a fundamental human trait that involves feeling empathy and understanding for others, and taking action to alleviate their suffering. It involves a deep sense of caring and concern for the well-being of others, regardless of their background, beliefs, or circumstances.

Charity

Charity refers to the act of giving or donating resources, time, or money to support a cause or organization that helps others in need. It is an essential aspect of social responsibility and can have a significant impact on individuals and communities.

About us and Our lead group

This team is responsible for sourcing, preparing, and distributing food to individuals or groups who are experiencing food insecurity or hunger. This lead group consist of individuals with skills in logistics, food preparation, finance, and communication, among others.
